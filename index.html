About Telos Project Ltd

Telos Project Ltd is a UK-registered company focused on the creation of online video content centred on motorcycle and mechanical projects, along with the development of small specialist products. We aim to produce honest, practical films that document real-world builds, experiments, and workshop techniques.

The company is currently pre-trading and preparing to commence activity in 2026.

Activities

  • Digital content production (video)
  • Online advertising partnerships
  • Future sale of specialist products via online platforms

Planned Projects

  • £2,500 Aprilia RSV4 Rebuild

    A full rebuild series documenting the reality of buying one of the cheapest RSV4s in the UK. From teardown to rebuild, mistakes included. Viewers help shape the direction — track or road, modifications, fixes, and priorities.

  • AliExpress Motorcycle Experiments

    Testing how far cheap parts can really go. Fairings, components, and eventually entire builds using AliExpress parts. Honest reviews, real-world fitment, and no sugar coating.

  • DIY vs OEM Tools

    Designing and 3D-printing specialist motorcycle tools that OEMs charge ridiculous money for. Comparing £150 official tools against £5 DIY alternatives.

  • Workshop Reviews & Problem Solving

    Practical reviews of tools, stands, and workshop gear used during the builds. Focused on what actually helps, not marketing hype.

  • Track Day Journey

    Taking rebuilt bikes onto the track for the first time. Sharing nerves, mistakes, learning moments, and what it is really like bringing a road bike onto circuit.

  • Extreme & Experimental Builds

    Longer-term, more ambitious projects including unusual engine swaps, unconventional ideas, and builds that may or may not work. The point is exploration, not perfection.
